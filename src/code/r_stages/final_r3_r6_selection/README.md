# Final R3 vs R6 Model Selection

- **Purpose**: Compares validation metrics and multi-year trajectory consistency of R3 and R6 candidates.
- **Input**: Long-format validation metrics and aggregated prediction datasets.
- **Output**: Model selection ranking tables and the recommended best scenario model designation.
- **Model Group**: Evaluation of recursive and multi-output variants of Ridge, RandomForest, and HistGradientBoosting.
- **Relationship to Other Stages**: Consolidated conclusion: R3 and R6 produced similar final scores, and R3 was chosen as the final scenario modeling architecture due to superior stability and robustness.
